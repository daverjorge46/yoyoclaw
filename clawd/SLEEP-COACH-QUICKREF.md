## The 5 Sleep Diary Questions (Track Daily)

1. What time did you get in bed?
2. What time did you try to fall asleep (lights out)?
3. How long did it take to fall asleep?
4. How many times did you wake up? For how long?
5. What time did you wake up for good? Get out of bed?

## Sleep Restriction Quick Math

**Starting PTIB (Prescribed Time In Bed)**:
- Average sleep time from baseline week
- Minimum = 5 hours (safety floor)
- Fixed wake time - PTIB = Bedtime

**Weekly Adjustment**:
| Sleep Efficiency | Action |
|-----------------|--------|
| < 85% | Cut 15 min from PTIB |
| 85-90% | Keep same |
| > 90% + rested | Add 15 min to PTIB |

## Sleep Hygiene Checklist

**Light**
- [ ] Morning bright light within 30 min
- [ ] Dim lights 2 hours before bed
- [ ] Screens off or blue-filtered 1 hour before bed

**Environment**
- [ ] Cool room (65-68Â°F)
- [ ] Dark (curtains/eye mask)
- [ ] Quiet (white noise/earplugs)

**Substances**
- [ ] Caffeine cut off 8+ hours before bed
- [ ] No alcohol as sleep aid
- [ ] No nicotine near bedtime
- [ ] Heavy meals finished 3+ hours before bed

**Schedule**
- [ ] Consistent wake time (weekends too)
- [ ] Wind-down routine 30-60 min before bed
- [ ] Bedtime only when sleepy

## One-Liner Interventions

**For resistance to getting out of bed:**
> "The 20-minute rule protects your bed-sleep connection. Temporary discomfort, permanent gain."

**For worry about tomorrow:**
> "What's the evidence that one bad night ruins your day? You've survived them before."

**For daytime sleepiness during restriction:**
> "Sleepiness means it's working. Your sleep drive is building."

**For weekend schedule slip:**
> "Monday's sleep starts Sunday morning. Keep the wake time."

**For "I tried everything":**
> "Have you tried CBT-I consistently for 6-8 weeks? It's the gold standard for a reason."
