## The Problem
No TTS solution handles Algerian Arabic (Darija) or Algerian French properly. Existing tools sound robotic and foreign — they don't capture the local accent, slang, or mixed-language patterns real Algerians use.

## The Solution
A TTS platform specifically tuned for the Algerian market. Supports Arabic + French with authentic local pronunciation. Useful for content creators, marketers, businesses, and anyone who needs audio content in Algerian voices.

## Target Market
- **Who:** Algerian content creators, marketers, e-commerce sellers, social media managers
- **Where:** Algeria-first, Maghreb expansion potential
- **Channels:** Facebook, Instagram, WhatsApp marketing
