## Temperature Key
- **Hot:** Ready to buy, needs proposal
- **Warm:** Interested, needs nurturing
- **Cold:** Showed interest once, gone quiet
- **Dead:** Said no or ghosted

## Follow-up Rules
- New lead → respond within 2 hours (WhatsApp)
- Warm lead with no response in 3 days → follow up
- Proposal sent with no response in 5 days → follow up once
- After 2 follow-ups with no response → mark cold, move on

## Client Communication Templates

### First Response (Darija)
```

[Personalized line about their business]
واش تقدر تعطيني شوية détails على واش تحتاج بالضبط؟
```
