| Chicken breast | 100g | 31g | 165 | Grilled is best |

## Simple Meal Ideas
### Quick Breakfast Options
- 3 eggs + bread + cheese
- Yogurt + oats + honey
- Egg sandwich (khobz + eggs + tomato)

### Lunch Ideas
- Grilled chicken + rice + salad
- Lentil soup (3das) + bread
- Tuna salad + bread
- Chicken shawarma plate (homemade)

### Dinner Ideas
- Sardines + salad + bread
- Eggs + vegetables
- Soup + bread (light)

### Snacks
- Yogurt
- Nuts (mixed)
- Fruit (banana, apple)
- Cheese + bread

## Foods to Limit
- Sugary drinks (soda, juice)
- Excessive bread (khobz trap — easy to overeat)
- Fried food (limit, don't eliminate)
- Late-night heavy meals

- Ramadan will require adjusted meal timing (suhoor/iftar planning)
- Cooking at home > eating out for macro control
