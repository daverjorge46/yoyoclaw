## Habit Building Rules
- Start with MAX 3 new habits at a time
- Don't try to be perfect — 80% consistency beats 100% for a week then quitting
- If a habit keeps failing, make it smaller (e.g., "1 glass of water" not "3 liters")
- Mark the habit tracker at the END of the day, not in advance
