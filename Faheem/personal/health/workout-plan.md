## Warm-Up (Before Every Session)
1. 5 min light cardio (walking, jumping jacks, cycling)
2. Arm circles — 10 each direction
3. Leg swings — 10 each leg
4. Hip circles — 10 each direction
5. 10 bodyweight squats
6. 5 push-ups (slow)

## Cool-Down / Stretching (After Every Session)
1. Quad stretch — 30s each leg
2. Hamstring stretch — 30s each leg
3. Chest doorway stretch — 30s
4. Child's pose — 30s
5. Hip flexor stretch — 30s each side
6. 5 deep breaths

## Progressive Overload Rules
- If you can do all reps with good form → increase weight next session
- Increase by smallest increment available (usually 2.5kg)
- If you can't complete the reps, stay at the same weight
- Track everything in `workout-log.md`
